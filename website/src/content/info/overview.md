# Overview

Welcome aboard. This event will be split into 3 parts!

## 1. Design & build projects (May 1st-July 31st)

The first step is designing and building hardware projects! Submit designs to collect funding. Build them to collect points.

**Collect at least 12 points to qualify to the event.** Get more points for more travel stipend!

When you submit your project, you can submit your project for one of 3 different tiers:

#### Tier 3, trinkets & small items! ($35 total) 4 pts

Get up to $25 for the initial design, and then $10 to make changes after building your project!

#### Tier 2, gadgets n' gizmos! ($150 total) 12 pts

Get up to $120 for the initial design, and then $30 to make changes after building your project!

#### Tier 1, machines and arcane science ($350 total) 20 pts

Get up to $300 for the initial design, and then $50 to make changes after building your project!

_feel like this funding isn't enough? dm @alexren!_

**To qualify for Open Sauce, you'll need to collect at least 12 points! This also comes with a $75 USD travel stipend.** Every extra 4 points (on top of the original 12) qualifies you for another $75 USD domestic / 150 USD int'l

Needs based stipends available!

## 2. Sauceathon! (July 15-17th)

After collecting 12 points, bring your projects and join 70 other teenagers for the very first teen-only sauceathon. The pitch? For the next 3 days, build the booth that you'll demo at Open Sauce.

## 3. Open Sauce! (July 18th-19th)

You and your teammates will be demoing at 1 of 20 booths in a special Hack-Club only section at Open Sauce! Good luck!
