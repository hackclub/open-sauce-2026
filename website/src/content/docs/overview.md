## Overview / qualifying

To qualify for Open Saucem you'll need to collect at least 3 points

When you submit your project, you can submit your project for one of 3 different tiers:

### Tier 3, trinkets & small items! ($30 max) | build 3 to qualify for Open Sauce!

### Tier 2, gadgets n' gizmos! ($150 max) | qualify for Open Sauce with a $75 travel stipend

These are where most project

### Tier 1, machines and arcane science | qualify for open sauce with a $200 Domestic/ $300 int'l travel stipend!

If you're trying to level up as a maker this year, this is where you want to go.

The first step is to come up with an idea.
